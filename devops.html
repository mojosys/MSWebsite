DevOps

DevOps practices let companies experience the power of rallying entire teams behind a defined process that embraces inclusivity and drives productivity. It’s not just one thing; it’s many efforts in concert that makes DevOps so powerful. Because how well your team develops software can make or break your business in a market. Robust DevOps practice increases the velocity and stability of software deployments while also reducing failure recovery time

DevOps Transformation Model

  1. Assessment phase will help in identifying the existing gaps in terms of process and practices
  2. Strategy & Implementation analysing the approach & provide solution and implement it with best practices
  3. Training & Coaching will focus on providing appropriate training and nurture team skills on Tools and Agile Methodology
  4. Monitoring phase will oversee the team and address any of the challenges they face
